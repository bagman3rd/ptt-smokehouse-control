# Build 6.6.0 — Eight-Number Kitchen EOD

Build 6.6.0 simplifies daily kitchen closeout without removing the existing detailed End-of-Day form.

## Quick EOD block

The top of the End-of-Day page now asks for only:

- Service date
- Sealed, unopened brisket units
- Pounds remaining from opened brisket
- Sealed, unopened pork units
- Pounds remaining from opened pork
- Sealed, unopened chicken units
- Pounds remaining from opened chicken
- Sealed, unopened rib units
- Pounds remaining from opened ribs
- Submit EOD Report

## Next-load credit rules

- Sealed pork units: credited against the next load.
- Sealed chicken units: credited against the next load.
- Sealed rib units: credited against the next load.
- Sealed brisket units: recorded for repurposed menu items, but not credited against the next brisket load.
- Opened-meat pounds for all four proteins: recorded for tacos, nachos, brisket bombs, and similar uses, but not credited against the next smoker load.

The detailed EOD form remains below the quick-entry section.
