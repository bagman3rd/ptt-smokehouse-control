# Support Operations — Founding Customer Phase

Support is intentionally human-led during the pilot. This avoids presenting an untested AI support agent as operational coverage.

- Severity 1: production unavailable or tenant-data exposure — immediate owner notification and deployment freeze.
- Severity 2: cook-plan/EOD workflow blocked — same business-day response.
- Severity 3: reporting, setup, or usability issue — response within two business days.
- Every incident receives a ticket, owner, timeline, resolution, and prevention note.
- At 10 active customers, add a second trained support operator.
- At 25 active customers, add documented on-call rotation and status-page communication.
- At 50 active customers, implement searchable support automation backed by approved documentation and human escalation.
